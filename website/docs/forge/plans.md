## Generation Usage Explained

### What Counts as a Generation?

**Each of these uses 1 generation:**
- Initial game creation from template
- Iteration prompt (adding features, fixing bugs, tweaking values)
- Regeneration after error or failed compile

**These do NOT count as generations:**
- Viewing source code
- Downloading project files
- Pushing to GitHub
- Publishing to store
- Updating store listing

### How to Maximize Your Generations

#### On Free Plan (5/month)

**Plan your game in advance:**
- Sketch mechanics and features on paper
- Write detailed prompts to get more done per generation
- Test thoroughly in preview before iterating

**Example efficient workflow:**
```
Gen 1: "Create a platformer with jump, move, and basic platforms.
        Add 3 levels with increasing difficulty."
Gen 2: "Add collectible coins and enemy spikes. Show score and lives."
Gen 3: "Polish: Add sound effects, particle effects on coin collect,
        and a victory screen."
Gen 4: Fix any bugs found during playtesting.
Gen 5: Reserve for final polish or emergency fix.
```

#### On Creator Plan (100/month)

**Iterate freely but stay organized:**
- 1-2 generations per feature is typical
- Reserve 20-30 gens for bug fixes and polish
- Budget 70 gens for new features and content

**Example monthly budget:**
- Game 1: 40 gens (build + publish)
- Game 2: 35 gens (early development)
- Maintenance: 15 gens (updates to existing games)
- Buffer: 10 gens (unexpected needs)

#### On Studio Plan (Unlimited)

**No need to ration ‚Äî experiment freely:**
- Try multiple approaches to the same feature
- A/B test different mechanics
- Iterate daily or hourly based on playtests
- Don't worry about "wasting" generations

### Generation Rollover

**Free Plan:** Unused generations expire at month end (no rollover)
**Creator Plan:** Up to 20 generations roll over to next month (max 120 total)
**Studio Plan:** Unlimited, no rollover needed

## Upgrading and Downgrading

### Upgrading

**From Free to Creator:**
- Instant activation
- Keep all existing projects
- Gain immediate access to publishing and commercial license
- Current month gets full 100 generations (prorated)

**From Creator to Studio:**
- Instant activation
- Generations become unlimited
- Priority builds and support activate immediately
- Invite team members right away

### Downgrading

**From Creator to Free:**
- Takes effect at next billing cycle
- Keeps commercial license for already-published games
- Can't publish new games
- Generations cap at 5/month

**From Studio to Creator:**
- Takes effect at next billing cycle
- Loses unlimited generations (caps at 100)
- Team seats disabled (additional seats removed)
- Priority features disabled

**Note:** Downgrading doesn't delete projects or unpublish games.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What happens if I hit my generation limit?

**Free Plan:** Wait until next month, or upgrade to Creator/Studio
**Creator Plan:** Wait until next month, or upgrade to Studio for unlimited

You can always download your existing projects and continue development locally (with manual compilation).

### Can I buy extra generations?

Not on Free plan. Creator plan can purchase add-on packs:
- 25 generations: $10
- 50 generations: $18
- 100 generations: $30

Add-ons expire at month end (no rollover).

### Does publishing use generations?

No. Publishing, updating games, and store management don't consume generations. Only code generation and iteration use your quota.

### If I cancel, do I lose my games?

No. Published games remain on the store. Downloaded source code is yours forever. GitHub repositories are unaffected. You just can't create new games or iterations.

### Can multiple people share one Creator account?

Technically possible, but against terms of service. Use Studio plan for teams ‚Äî it's designed for collaboration with proper seat management.

### Do I get charged for team seats that aren't used?

No. You're only charged for active seats. Invite someone, they count as a seat. Remove them, you're not charged next cycle.

### What if I need more than 5 Studio seats?

Additional seats are $10/month each. Add unlimited seats from your account dashboard.

### Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?

Yes, but you can't get a refund on unused monthly time. Annual switch takes effect at next billing cycle.
