# Content Guidelines

MonoPlay maintains content policies to ensure a safe, legal, and high-quality platform for players worldwide. All games must comply with these guidelines.

## General Principles

### 1. Legality

All content must comply with:

- Applicable laws in your jurisdiction
- Applicable laws where MonoPlay operates
- International copyright and trademark law
- Platform-specific regulations (Windows, macOS, Linux policies)

Publishers are responsible for ensuring their content is legal.

### 2. Authenticity

Games must:

- Be original work or properly licensed
- Not impersonate other games, brands, or developers
- Accurately represent gameplay in screenshots and descriptions
- Disclose all in-game monetization clearly

### 3. Safety

Games must not:

- Contain malware, spyware, or viruses
- Steal user data or cryptocurrency
- Exploit players financially or emotionally
- Harm player devices or networks

Security scanning enforces most safety requirements automatically.

## Prohibited Content

The following content is **strictly prohibited** and will result in immediate rejection:

### Illegal Content

- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Content promoting terrorism or extremist violence
- Human trafficking or exploitation
- Non-consensual intimate imagery
- Illegal drugs or controlled substances (region-dependent)
- Illegal gambling (unlicensed real-money gambling)

### Malicious Software

- Malware, viruses, trojans, ransomware
- Cryptocurrency miners (without explicit disclosure and opt-in)
- Keyloggers or data harvesting tools
- Remote access trojans (RATs)
- Any software designed to harm users or devices

### Intellectual Property Violations

- Pirated or cracked software
- Unauthorized use of copyrighted material
- Trademark infringement
- Fan games using copyrighted characters without license
- Mods or content requiring pirated base games

### Deceptive Practices

- Fake games or apps
- Misleading screenshots or descriptions
- Hidden costs not disclosed upfront
- Fake reviews or manipulated ratings
- Impersonation of other developers or brands

## Restricted Content

The following content is **allowed with restrictions**:

### Adult Content

**Allowed if:**
- Properly age-gated (Adults Only rating)
- Does not violate other policies (consent, legality)
- Clearly disclosed in metadata
- Complies with regional laws

**Not Allowed:**
- Sexual content involving minors (real or depicted)
- Non-consensual sexual content
- Content illegal in major jurisdictions

### Violence

**Allowed if:**
- Age rating accurately reflects violence level
- Excessive gore disclosed in content descriptors
- Context is clear (warfare, horror, fantasy, etc.)

**Not Allowed:**
- Realistic depictions of torture without artistic merit
- Content glorifying real-world violence or mass shootings
- Content designed solely to shock or disgust

### Gambling

**Allowed if:**
- Simulated gambling only (no real-money payouts)
- Properly age-rated (Teen or Mature)
- Odds disclosed for loot boxes/gacha mechanics
- Not designed to exploit gambling addiction

**Not Allowed:**
- Real-money gambling without proper licensing
- Conversion of in-game currency to real money
- Unregulated betting or casino operations

### Cryptocurrency Integration

**Allowed if:**
- Clearly disclosed in game description
- Does not access user wallets without explicit permission
- No misleading promises of profit
- Complies with securities regulations

**Not Allowed:**
- Wallet theft or private key harvesting
- Pump-and-dump schemes
- Unregistered securities offerings
- Misleading investment promises

### Mods and User-Generated Content

**Allowed if:**
- Base game allows modding
- You have rights to distribute the content
- Users are warned about third-party content risks
- Moderation tools provided for multiplayer UGC

**Not Allowed:**
- Mods requiring pirated games
- Bypassing DRM or copy protection
- Cheats for online multiplayer games
- Unauthorized asset rips from other games

## Age Ratings

Accurate age ratings are mandatory:

### Everyone (E)

Content suitable for all ages:

- No violence beyond mild cartoon violence
- No sexual content
- No profanity
- No gambling, alcohol, or drug references

**Examples**: Puzzle games, family-friendly platformers, educational games

### Teen (T)

Content suitable for ages 13+:

- Mild to moderate violence
- Infrequent use of profanity
- Suggestive themes (no explicit content)
- Simulated gambling

**Examples**: Action games, competitive multiplayer, adventure games

### Mature (M)

Content suitable for ages 17+:

- Intense violence or gore
- Strong language
- Sexual content (non-explicit)
- Real-money gambling simulation
- Alcohol and drug references

**Examples**: First-person shooters, horror games, mature RPGs

### Adults Only (AO)

Content suitable for ages 18+:

- Explicit sexual content
- Extreme violence
- Real-money gambling (where legal)

**Examples**: Adult visual novels, explicit horror, casino games

**Note**: AO games have distribution restrictions on some platforms.

## Content Descriptors

Select all applicable descriptors:

- Violence (cartoon, fantasy, realistic)

- Sexual Content (mild, moderate, explicit)

- Strong Language
- Alcohol Reference

Accurate descriptors help players make informed choices and comply with regional regulations.

## DMCA and Copyright Complaints

### For Copyright Holders

If you believe content on MonoPlay infringes your copyright:

1. Send DMCA notice to: dmca@monoplay.xyz
2. Include required information (17 U.S.C. ยง 512(c)):
 - Your contact information
 - Identification of copyrighted work
 - Identification of infringing material
 - Good faith statement
 - Accuracy statement
 - Physical or electronic signature

### For Publishers

If your game receives a DMCA complaint:

1. You'll receive email notification with complaint details
2. Game may be temporarily delisted pending review
3. You can file a counter-notice if you dispute the claim
4. Resolution timeline: 10-14 business days

**Repeated copyright violations may result in account suspension.**

## Delisting and Removal

Games may be delisted for:

- Policy violations discovered after approval
- Legal requests (DMCA, court orders)
- Security vulnerabilities not fixed within 30 days
- Abandonment (no publisher response to critical issues)
- Repeated player complaints validated by review

**Delisting Process:**

1. Warning email sent to publisher (except for critical security/legal issues)
2. 7-day period to address issues
3. Game delisted if unresolved
4. Appeal process available

**Impact of Delisting:**

- Game removed from catalog
- Existing licenses remain valid (players keep access)
- No new purchases allowed
- GRID nodes stop seeding new downloads
- Publisher can fix issues and request relisting

## Appeals and Disputes

Disagree with a content decision?

1. Submit appeal via Developer Console
2. Include detailed explanation and supporting evidence
3. Review by senior content moderation team
4. Response within 5 business days
5. Final decision binding

**Valid Grounds for Appeal:**

- Factual error in review
- Misinterpretation of policy
- Context not considered
- Similar content approved elsewhere

**Not Valid:**

- Disagreement with policy itself
- "Other platforms allow it"
- Claiming censorship

## Regional Restrictions

Some content may be restricted in specific regions:

**Examples:**
- Nazi imagery (illegal in Germany)
- Gambling (varies by country)
- Political content (varies by country)
- LGBT themes (restricted in some countries)

**Publisher Options:**

- Geo-restrict game in specific regions
- Create region-specific builds
- Remove content globally to maximize reach

MonoPlay provides tools to manage regional availability.

## Best Practices

### Before Submission

- Review these guidelines thoroughly
- Check age rating and descriptors match actual content
- Remove placeholder or copyrighted assets
- Verify you have rights to all included content
- Test that content matches screenshots and descriptions

### During Development

- Document licenses for all third-party assets
- Obtain necessary rights for music, sound effects, and art
- Implement content warnings for sensitive material
- Consider regional sensitivities for global release

### After Approval

- Monitor player feedback for content concerns
- Update age rating if content changes significantly
- Respond promptly to DMCA or legal requests
- Maintain accurate game descriptions
