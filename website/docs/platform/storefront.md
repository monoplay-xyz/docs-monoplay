# MonoPlay Storefront

The MonoPlay storefront is where you discover, browse, and purchase games. Built on blockchain technology, it offers a transparent, decentralized alternative to traditional game stores.

## Browsing Games

### Homepage

The MonoPlay homepage features curated content to help you discover new games:

**Featured Section:**
- Staff picks and editorial highlights
- Major new releases
- Limited-time promotions
- Seasonal recommendations

**Trending Now:**
- Games with the most recent purchases
- Rising stars in the community
- Real-time popularity rankings

**Top Rated:**
- Highest-rated games by the community
- Filtered by genre, release date, or popularity
- Minimum review threshold to prevent manipulation

**New Releases:**
- Games published in the last 30 days
- Sorted by release date (newest first)
- "Coming Soon" section for upcoming titles

**Free to Play:**
- Games with no upfront cost
- May include optional in-game purchases
- Perfect for trying MonoPlay risk-free

## Wishlists

Add games to your wishlist to track them over time:

**Benefits:**
- Get email/notification when price drops
- Alerts when wishlisted games go on sale
- Reminders for upcoming releases
- Share your wishlist with friends

**Managing Wishlist:**
1. Click "Add to Wishlist" on any game page
2. View your full wishlist at [monoplay.xyz/wishlist](https://monoplay.xyz/wishlist)
3. Sort by: Price, Release Date, Discount %
4. Remove games you're no longer interested in

**Wishlist Privacy:**
- Public: Anyone can view
- Friends only: Visible to connected wallets
- Private: Only you can see

## Collections & Bundles

**Curated Collections:**
MonoPlay staff and community members create themed collections:
- "Best Indie Games of 2025"
- "Couch Co-op Classics"
- "Games Under 5 LYTH"
- "Steam Deck Optimized"

**Developer Bundles:**

**Discounts:**

## Price Tracking & Sales

**Price History:**
Every game page shows historical pricing:
- Line graph of price over time
- Current price vs. all-time high/low
- Average sale discount percentage

**Sale Events:**
- Seasonal sales (Summer, Winter, Spring, etc.)
- Developer spotlights
- Genre-specific sales
- Flash sales (24-48 hours)

**Price Alerts:**
Set custom price targets:
1. Open a game page
2. Click "Set Price Alert"
3. Enter target price (e.g., "Notify me when under 15 LYTH")
4. Get notified via email or push notification

## Privacy & Data

**What We Track:**
- Your wallet address (public blockchain data)
- Games you've purchased (on-chain)
- Your wishlist (if you create one)
- Anonymous usage statistics (page views, search queries)

**What We Don't Track:**
- Email or personal information (unless you voluntarily provide it)
- Browsing history outside MonoPlay
- In-game activity or playtime (stored locally in launcher)

**Data Control:**
- Export your data anytime
- Delete your wishlist or review history
- Disconnect wallet to "log out"
